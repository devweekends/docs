## Where to search

- Wellfound (AngelList), YC Work at a Startup, RemoteOK, HN Who’s Hiring
- Company career pages (apply directly + reach out to engineers)

## Tips

- Showcase projects with LLMs/agents, infra (vector DBs, orchestration), and measurable outcomes.
- Add an AI section to your resume (models, frameworks, infra you’ve used).
