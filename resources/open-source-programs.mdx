## High-Impact Programs

These are the most recognized and competitive programs. Getting selected is a significant resume boost.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Google Summer of Code (GSoC)" icon="google" href="/resources/open-source-programs/gsoc">
    10–22 week program. Work on real open-source projects with mentorship. Stipend: $750–$6,600 (PPP-based). Open to anyone 18+ — not just students.
  </Card>
  <Card title="LFX Mentorship (Linux Foundation)" icon="linux" href="/resources/open-source-programs/lfx-mentorship">
    12-week cohorts running 3× per year. Focus on cloud-native projects (Kubernetes, Prometheus, etc.). Stipend: $3,000–$6,600.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Outreachy" icon="hand-holding-heart" href="/resources/open-source-programs/outreachy">
    Paid remote internships for underrepresented groups in tech. $7,000 stipend + $500 travel. Two cohorts per year (May & December).
  </Card>
  <Card title="MLH Fellowship" icon="users" href="/resources/open-source-programs/mlh-fellowship">
    12-week cohort program with 3 tracks (Open Source, SWE, SRE). Work in small pods with daily standups. Stipend: $1,000–$5,000 (need-based).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Summer of Bitcoin" icon="bitcoin-sign" href="/resources/open-source-programs/summer-of-bitcoin">
    Contribute to Bitcoin open-source projects. Developer and Designer tracks. Stipend: ~$6,600 paid in BTC. Strong alumni career outcomes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Google Season of Docs" icon="file-pen" href="/resources/open-source-programs/google-season-of-docs">
    Technical writing for open-source projects. Program concluded in 2024 — page covers legacy info and alternative documentation opportunities.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## More Opportunities

Smaller or more specialized programs that are excellent for building your open-source profile.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Open Source Promotion Plan (OSPP)" icon="globe" href="/resources/open-source-programs/ospp">
    Summer program by the Chinese Academy of Sciences. Open to students worldwide. Bonuses up to ¥12,000 RMB (~$1,650).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Linux Kernel Mentorship" icon="microchip" href="/resources/open-source-programs/linux-kernel-mentorship">
    Contribute to the Linux kernel under expert guidance. One of the most technically challenging and rewarding programs.
  </Card>
  <Card title="LF Networking Mentorship" icon="network-wired" href="/resources/open-source-programs/lfn-mentorship">
    Open-source networking and telecom projects (SDN, NFV, 5G). Part of the LFX ecosystem.
  </Card>
  <Card title="FOSSASIA Codeheat" icon="fire" href="/resources/open-source-programs/fossasia-codeheat">
    Beginner-friendly coding contest. Earn points by contributing to FOSSASIA projects. Prizes for top contributors.
  </Card>
  <Card title="24 Pull Requests" icon="gift" href="/resources/open-source-programs/24-pull-requests">
    Community challenge: one open-source contribution per day from Dec 1–24. Free, fun, and great for building habits.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
