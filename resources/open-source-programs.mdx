## High‑impact programs

- **Google Summer of Code (GSoC)** — flexible 10–22 weeks. Stipend varies by region.
  - Website: https://summerofcode.withgoogle.com/
  - Timeline: https://developers.google.com/open-source/gsoc/timeline
  - Stipend reference: https://developers.google.com/open-source/gsoc/help/student-stipends

- **Linux Foundation Mentorship (LFX)** — 12‑week cohorts year‑round.
  - Website: https://mentorship.lfx.linuxfoundation.org/
  - Timelines: https://docs.linuxfoundation.org/lfx/mentorship/mentorship-program-timelines
  - Stipend: https://docs.linuxfoundation.org/lfx/mentorship/mentee-stipends

- **Outreachy** — paid remote internships focused on underrepresented folks.
  - Website: https://www.outreachy.org/
  - Payments: https://www.outreachy.org/docs/internship/#payments

- **MLH Fellowship** — multiple tracks incl. open source; need‑based stipend.
  - Website: https://fellowship.mlh.io/

- **Google Season of Docs** — technical writing for OSS projects.
  - Website: https://developers.google.com/season-of-docs
  - Timeline: https://developers.google.com/season-of-docs/docs/timeline
