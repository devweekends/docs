## What is System Design?

System Design focuses on the **high-level architecture** of software systems. While LLD deals with classes and code, HLD deals with:

- **Components** and how they interact
- **Scalability** and handling growth
- **Reliability** and fault tolerance
- **Data storage** and retrieval patterns
- **Trade-offs** between different approaches

## Interview Approach

<Steps>
  <Step title="Clarify Requirements (5 min)">
    Ask about users, scale, features, and constraints
  </Step>
  <Step title="High-Level Design (10 min)">
    Draw main components and their interactions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deep Dive (15 min)">
    Detail specific components, discuss trade-offs
  </Step>
  <Step title="Address Bottlenecks (10 min)">
    Identify and solve scaling challenges
  </Step>
</Steps>
